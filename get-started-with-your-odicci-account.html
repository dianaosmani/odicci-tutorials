In this tutorial we will give you a step by step guide to each option in the Settings to help you get started with your Odicci account. This will include your account Profile, Company information, Users, Sites, Integrations and many more.

Everything we will cover in this section will be in the Settings page, to navigate there; click ‘ODICCI Admin’ on the top right-hand corner (accessible from anywhere in the platform) and select Settings .

How to create a Profile for your account where you can edit at any time.

  1. Assuming that you have navigated to the Settings  (ODICCI Admin > Settings), click on Profile on the sidebar on the left.
  2. Fill in all the fields and click Save.

How to add your company details and edit at any time

  1. Assuming that you have navigated to the Settings  (ODICCI Admin > Settings), click on Company on the sidebar on the left.
  2. Fill in all the fields and click Save.

How to add new location sites manually or import a list of multiple sites in one go, and manage existing sites

Adding a new Site

  1. Assuming that you have navigated to the Settings  (ODICCI Admin > Settings), click on Sites on the sidebar on the left.
  2. Click on Add New Site.
  3. Fill in all the fields and click Save.

Importing a list of Sites

  1. Assuming that you have navigated to the Settings  (ODICCI Admin > Settings), click on Sites on the sidebar on the left.
  2. Click on Import List.
  3. Choose your CSV file and click Import.

Managing existing Sites

  1. Management actions on sites include: View , Activate/Deactivate , Edit and Delete .
  2. You may also Search for sites using different search criteria from the drop down list.
